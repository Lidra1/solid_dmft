make_spaghetti.py

Script to combine the information from maxent_sigma.py and the dft_bands_input to generate the DMFT spectral function and DMFT band structure from Sigma(omega).

Known problems:
  • when using parameter energy_shift_orbitals in soliDMFT, this is not

accounted for in this routine. It requires a similar modification of the hopping matrix of SumkDFT as in dmft_cycle.py.

Author: Max Merkel, 2020-2021
